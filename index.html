Zhuowei (Jack) Li

Hi! I am Zhuowei (Jack) Li, currently a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Rutgers University under the supervision of Professor Dimitris N. Metaxas. Prior to this, I earned my Master’s degree from Columbia University, where I had the privilege of working under the guidance of Professors Andrew Laine and Shih-Fu Chang. I also spent two years as a Computer Vision Researcher at SenseTime, working with Professor Shaoting Zhang. My Bachelor’s degree was completed at Northeastern University, China.

My broad research interests span Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Medical Image Analysis. Off-late, I am of especial interest in foundention models with a focus on Large Language Model (LLM) and Multi-modal Learning.
